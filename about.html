I was born in India – a small town in the state of Uttar Pradesh. I come from a middle-class family and struggled with various aspects of daily life. I used to excel in school but struggled with identity, but with time, I learned to embrace a kind of primitive adaptability.

Growing up in the suburbs, I spent four years of my education transitioning from introvert to extrovert. In college, I explored drama, music, journalism, coding, humanitarian services, robotics, and entrepreneurship apart from my engineering studies.

I like to create, innovate and enable creation. Enjoyed this while working on an idea about aggregating artists on one platform and providing them with gigs. Also for two years, I worked as an Entrepreneur in Residence at CodeAsylums. You can find more information in my technical section. I'm currently working on a few ideas and would love to connect for innovation, discussion, and ideas, if you have any.

I've grown to love the mountains, which I now call my home.
